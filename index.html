Why We Started

There is a significant gap in the industry whereby excellent quality of design and execution is inaccessible to most customers. This is inherently due to a lack of professionally qualified and experienced people in the industry. The cost of a great design and execution is very high and out of budget for many customers.



Less than twenty companies in Southwestern India of our size And expertise undertake End-to-End works, create highly Desirable Design, and execute Them.


The problem is that these Companies are inaccessible to most of the customers looking to get interiors of their home done as they charge a very hefty premium for their capability and service and take up projects with high budgets. Therefore only the customers with deep pockets approach them and get their work done. This makes most customers, looking to get their home's interiors done, go to much less capable and cheaper options, like freelance interior designers and contractors who are designers in name only. Unsurprisingly, they fail to provide the design and high standards of execution that big firms offer.
Due to our transparent and ethical pricing, you pay much less for the work, which would have otherwise cost 35-65% more.

Our capabilities enable us to create and execute world-class designs which are not available to a customer when they choose a lesser capable/equipped company for their work, as the customer's budget does not meet the quotations of the more prominent companies who have top talent and highly Skilled teams to develop and execute the high-end and wellThought designs, and hence charge premiums.

Our goal is to provide our customers access to the best of designing, production and execution, at a fraction of the cost by being transparent, ethical, having lower prices than the competition, and having as big and focused teams as the competition for each of the verticals required to complete your home.
